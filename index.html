About Me

Hello! I'm Michael, a Computer Science student based out of Anaheim, CA and attending Irvine Valley College. I've been learning about computer science since I was twelve when I started programming with arduino. Since then I have taken classes on simple front end development (hence this site), Java, Python, C, and C++. Along with this I have taken care to teach myself Javascript, Git, and lots of useful things that my classes didn't teach for instance, version control.
